What is the SHARP Framework for Local Government?

SHARP is a comprehensive solution for managing staff housing and critical assets in remote regions. For local governments, this framework extends to delivering essential community services, ensuring quality, sustainability, and efficiency across all operations. It focuses on five pillars: Staff (meeting diverse needs), Housing (managing lifecycle), Assets (data-driven decisions), Resources (training for limited settings), and Planning (collaboration and adaptability). Supported by integrated technologies, SHARP delivers measurable outcomes for both internal operations and external community services.
